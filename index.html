Help & FAQ

Need help?

Get in touch and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

support@clipento.com →
How do I create a bookmark?

While watching a video, tap the bookmark button to save the current moment. You can then name it, add tags and organise it into sessions.

How do I organise my videos?

Use Sessions and Folders to group your videos by workshop, teacher or dance style. Tap the + button to create a new session or folder.

Does Clipento upload my videos?

No. Your videos always stay on your device. Only your bookmarks and organisational data can optionally be synced to the cloud.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) account. Go to your account settings and select Subscriptions to manage or cancel.

How do I delete my data?

To delete your local data, simply delete the App. To request deletion of any cloud-synced data, email us at support@clipento.com and we will process your request within 30 days.

Which video formats does Clipento support?

Clipento works with videos stored in your device's camera roll and photo library. Any video format supported by your device is supported by Clipento.

Who we are

Clipento is developed and operated by Lithe Studios Ltd, a company registered in England and Wales.

Contact: support@clipento.com

What data we collect

Clipento is designed with privacy at its core. The app works entirely on your device and does not require an account. We may collect the following to keep the app running smoothly:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Anonymous usage analytics to help us improve the app — you can turn this off at any time in Settings → Privacy

What data we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name or personal identity
  • We do not access your camera or microphone
  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not access or upload your videos
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • Bookmarks, tags, sessions and notes you create stay on your device — we never see them

Analytics & opt-out

We use PostHog (EU-hosted, GDPR-compliant) to collect anonymous usage analytics. This helps us understand which features are used most so we can make Clipento better. Analytics data is completely anonymous — it never includes your videos, bookmarks, tags, notes, or any content you create.

What analytics data looks like:

  • Aggregated feature-use counts (e.g. "learning mode was started")
  • Bucketed durations and counts (e.g. "session had 5–10 bookmarks")
  • Device OS and app version
  • Your subscription tier (free / pro)

What it never includes:

  • Video filenames, paths, or content
  • Bookmark titles, descriptions, or annotations
  • Tag, folder, or playlist names
  • Any free-form text you type
  • Your identity or contact details

To opt out: open the app, go to Settings → Privacy and turn off the Analytics toggle. When disabled, no data is sent and any locally queued events are immediately deleted. You can re-enable analytics at any time.

Cloud sync (optional)

If you choose to enable cloud sync, your bookmarks, tags, sessions and app data are securely stored using Google Firebase Firestore. Your video files are never uploaded to the cloud. Only your organisational data is synced.

In-app purchases & subscriptions

Clipento offers optional Pro subscription upgrades processed through Apple App Store and Google Play. We do not handle or store your payment information. All payments are processed by Apple or Google directly.

Subscription management and cancellation is handled through your App Store or Google Play account settings.

Third party services

  • PostHog (EU) — Anonymous usage analytics (opt-out available in Settings → Privacy)
  • Google Firebase (Firestore) — Optional cloud sync of app data
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — App distribution and in-app purchases

Data retention

Locally stored data remains on your device until you delete the App or manually clear your data. If you use cloud sync, your data is retained in Firebase until you request deletion. Contact support@clipento.com to request deletion — we will process your request within 30 days.

Children's privacy

Clipento is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your rights (UK & GDPR)

If you are located in the United Kingdom or European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and port your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@clipento.com.
